# Suggested Features for Sixth Gear

Here are 15 features to consider implementing to further enhance the productivity and knowledge management capabilities of Sixth Gear:

1. **Habit Tracker:** A new DocType to track daily/weekly habits with a streak counter and visualization graph.
2. **Morning/Evening Review:** Automated "Review" wizards that prompt you to plan your day or reflect on achievements.
3. **Pomodoro Timer:** A built-in timer in the Task View that logs actual time spent against the task.
4. **Readwise Integration:** Automatically sync highlights from Kindle/Instapaper into the `Note` DocType.
5. **Recurring Tasks:** Enhanced logic for tasks that repeat (e.g., "Pay Rent" every month).
6. **Weekly Review Dashboard:** A specialized view to clear the Inbox, review Active Projects, and check Someday/Maybe lists (GTD style).
7. **Zettelkasten Linking:** Visual graph view showing connections between Notes based on links and tags.
8. **Email-to-Inbox:** A feature to forward emails to a specific address and have them appear as `Capture Items`.
9. **Voice Memos:** Mobile-friendly audio recording in the `Capture Item` that auto-transcribes to text (using OpenAI Whisper).
10. **Goal Tracking (OKRs):** A higher-level "Goal" DocType that sits above Projects to track Objectives and Key Results.
11. **Browser Extension:** A Chrome/Firefox extension to clip URLs and text directly to the Sixth Gear Inbox.
12. **Mood Tracker:** A simple daily prompt to log mood and energy levels, correlated with productivity stats.
13. **Gamification:** XP points and levels based on completed tasks and maintained habits.
14. **Focus Mode:** A button that hides all sidebars and distractions, showing only the current Task or Note.
15. **Offline Support:** PWA (Progressive Web App) capabilities to allow capturing and viewing data without an internet connection.
